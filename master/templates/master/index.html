Our story from founder Andrew Burrill.

Rainbows n' stuff.

Chateau de Beau was born out of a University of Virginia class project to create a unique and couture Bow Tie for the annual Foxfield Races in Charlottesville, Virginia. Originally starting out selling a few dozen every week to friends, has escalated to an unbelievable amount of orders in the last year. Chateau de Beau is proud to always be American made using only the highest quality fabrics and innovative thinking practices. Our purpose is to enable our customers to stand out with our unique styles and patterns. We find great joy in helping to cultivate individual confidence and identity through our artistry.
